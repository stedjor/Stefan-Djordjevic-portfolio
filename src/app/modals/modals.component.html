Information about project

Whole project was written in Angular Framework version 8 with some extra tools. I tried to use many different possibilities that Angular provides.
For the layout I used Bootstrap framework and Less preprocessor and for the animations I used JQuery framework. You are probably wondering why I didn't use Angular Material and Angular Animations, I was guided by fact that "the fastest / shortest way is the one you know", namely I don't have much experience but I will do my best to perfect it.
Since I don't know the backend programming languages so well, I decided to use the Firebase database and connect it to Angular.

FourSquare app

I got the idea for this app when I applied for a job at a Quantox company, I was tasked with setting up an application that would use FourSquare API and google maps. for this task I used JavaScript, JQuery and Ajax tools. since I worked in Angular framework in company Quantox I decided to create one in it using another Here WeGo map.
Instructions:
  1. You need to click the allow button for the app to find your location on the map,
  2. Then, in the upper right corner, you should enter the term places you want to find in search, for example a restaurant, coffee, shop, etc and press enter.
  3. On the left side you will see list of cards of all places near you up to two kilometers away, and the map will show markers of all places,
  4. Clicking on a card in the map will show a marker of that, also clicking on the marker card will place that spot,
  5. You can see the address and distance from your location on the card.

UsedCars App

Car Ads
This application was made only for presentation, all information is fabricated. If you want to try it feel free to use arbitrary information. It is an advertising application for used car sales. You can preview and create ads in it, you can only modify or delete your ads. You can also leave a comment on each ad.
In this app we have two pages: Car Ads and Create Ad.
Instructions for Car Ads page:
  1. On the left side are listings of ads. If you made an ad, you will have two lists, one with your ads and one with all others. There is also a filter button for filtering ads and Sort By... for sorting ads.
  2. Clicking on one ad will open a tab with details about that ad on the right if you have accessed the app on a larger screen or if you are on your mobile phone at the bottom.
  3. If you clicked on your ad, the top right of the tab will show two buttons for editing and deleting the ad.
  4. That card contains pictures, information and a section where you can read and write a comment.
  5. If you click on (Edit) a new page will open with all your information that you can change.

UsedCars App

Create Ad
I have created a few items in the Create Ad page for information to fill, there may be more, but this is enough for presentation.
Instructions for Create Ad page:
  1. While creating your ad you can add pictures, car information and information about yourself.
  2. If your model has an extra mark, click on the arrow to the right of the place for Model (the arrow points to the right) and a place will open where you can enter the mark of your car.
  3. Also If Brand or Model of your car is not on the list, click on the arrow between the Brand and the place for Model (the arrow points down) and two new places will open where you can enter the Brand and Model arbitrarily.
  4. All fields are required.
